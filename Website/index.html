Imperial Civilizations is a realistic Minecraft Roleplay server set in the 11th century Europe with the addition of Northern Africa, Russia, and Persia. We have multiple different plugins and gameplay alterations to help improve the experience for players, and to provide a realistic perspective into the 1100s. We strive to provide the best possible environment for our community, and we hope you'll be a part of it. Our server is well suited for both players new to RolePlay, and for the veterans who're used to it.
